# 🛡️ PRISM

### Privileged Risk Intelligence & Security Management Platform

> **An AI-assisted platform designed to detect insider threats, privileged access misuse, and anomalous privileged activities within the Bank of Maharashtra ecosystem.**

---

## 🏦 About PRISM

PRISM (Privileged Risk Intelligence & Security Management Platform) is a cybersecurity solution developed by **Team QRIUS** for the **FinSpark'26 Hackathon** organized by **Bank of Maharashtra**.

Modern banking systems rely on privileged users such as administrators, database managers, and IT personnel. While essential, these accounts also pose one of the highest insider security risks. Existing monitoring solutions often generate excessive alerts without sufficient operational context, making investigations slower and increasing false positives.

PRISM addresses this challenge by correlating privileged user activities with banking workflows, operational context, and evidence to provide intelligent, explainable, and actionable risk detection.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Detect and prevent insider threats and privileged access misuse within the Bank of Maharashtra ecosystem by enabling intelligent monitoring, evidence-based investigation, and contextual risk assessment.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

PRISM combines:

- Operational Blueprint Validation
- Evidence-Based Decision Validation
- Customer Impact Intelligence
- Real-Time Risk Correlation
- Explainable Risk Scoring

to identify suspicious privileged activities while minimizing false positives and improving investigation efficiency.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🛡️ Operational Blueprint Validation
Validates privileged activities against expected banking operational workflows to identify suspicious deviations rather than relying only on predefined rules.

---

## 🔍 Evidence-Based Decision Validation
Correlates user activities, transaction context, approvals, and operational evidence to provide explainable investigations and reduce false positives.

---

## ⚠️ Intelligent Risk Prioritization
Ranks security incidents based on contextual risk, privileged access level, operational impact, and customer sensitivity.

---

## 📊 Unified Investigation Workspace
Provides analysts with correlated events, evidence timelines, risk scores, and investigation history within a single interface.

---

## 📈 Real-Time Monitoring
Continuously monitors privileged accounts, administrative activities, authentication events, and critical banking operations.

---

## 📝 Audit & Compliance Support
Maintains complete audit trails and investigation records to support regulatory compliance and forensic analysis.
