## рџЏ¦ About PRISM

PRISM (Privileged Risk Intelligence & Security Management Platform) is a cybersecurity solution developed by **Team QRIUS** for the **FinSpark'26 Hackathon** organized by **Bank of Maharashtra**.

Modern banking systems rely on privileged users such as administrators, database managers, and IT personnel. While essential, these accounts also pose one of the highest insider security risks. Existing monitoring solutions often generate excessive alerts without sufficient operational context, making investigations slower and increasing false positives.

PRISM addresses this challenge by correlating privileged user activities with banking workflows, operational context, and evidence to provide intelligent, explainable, and actionable risk detection.
